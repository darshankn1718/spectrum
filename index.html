    About Spectrum

    We Are

    Spectrum Tool Engineers was started in the year 1990 by Mr. V. Sudarshan a Technocrat with a Expertise of Die and Mould making obtained from a reputed institute. Design and development of Tools for Precision Engineering Industries is our forte. Over the years, we have evolved to be a premier player in the field of Design, Mould making, Injection Moulded plastic part production, Sub-assemblies, Mechanical parts production and complete product development. We are focused on delivering cost effective solutions that meet the strictest of industry standards. We provide end-to-end tooling solutions for a diverse range of Industries such as Telecom, Electrical, Automotive, Electronics, Health Care, LED Lighting, Gaming industry, etc. In addition to moulds, we manufacture components, sub-assemblies and complete products for our customers.
