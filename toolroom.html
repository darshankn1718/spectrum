Design & Development

Spectrum strongly believe that, good designs followed by sound engineering practices and effective document control are key for successful completion of projects on agreed time line. We have experienced Engineers who will ensure designs which are robust and cost effective to meet customer specified performance and reliability expectations.

We have installed concurrent CAD / CAM software to receive inputs electronically and effectively interact with customer counterparts. We can receive 3D part files and other design inputs in

  • PTC- Creo  
  • Translation formats like IGES or STEP 
  • All our tool designs are done in 3D and detail drawings in 2D.

We have the following capabilities

  • Proto Tools / Low and Medium production tools / High volume multi cavity tools.  
  • Two plate moulds, hot runner moulds / Moulds with hydraulic side cores / spring cores/ multi stage ejection / Moulds with injection and ejection on fixed side.  
  • Services in 3D scanning / GD &T / Mould flow analysis to support product design & reverse engineering.

Mould Manufacturing

Mould making facility is spread over approximately 6000 sqft built area with facilities as below,

  • 3 axes CNC Vertical Machining Centre with Makino and Prodis
  • CNC -EDM –Machines. Agie Charmiles, Oscar Max, Electronica
  • WEDM –Machines- Agie Charmiles, Electronica
  • Precision grinding machines – Kent,
  • Conventional Milling machines
  • Video measuring system for effectively measuring the steel elements / electrode and moulded parts


We use internationally accepted tool steels / accessories / Hot Runner systems and other special elements apart from using best engineering practices in our mould making.
